English

This privacy policy describes how the mobile application Blip Blip - Hourly Chime handles information.

  • The app does not collect personal data from you. There is no login or user account.
  • The app uses Google AdMob to show advertisements. AdMob and its partners may process data as described in Google’s policies, including device identifiers used for advertising and fraud prevention. See Google’s Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Apart from what AdMob may collect as part of serving ads, the app does not use analytics, tracking SDKs, or behavioral profiling.
  • The app stores only local preferences on your device (for example chime settings and language choice) using Android SharedPreferences. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to the developer’s servers by the app.
  • This app is not directed at children and is not designed as a children’s app; no COPPA-specific collection is intended.

If you have questions about this policy, contact yriftt@gmail.com.
